Random Selection

FMCSA requires random testing of safety-sensitive employees at a specific amount per year. This testing must be evenly spread throughout the year and the selection process must be made by a scientifically valid method.

Pre-employment

A pre-employment drug screen is required for driver applicants or current employees transferring to a CDL position. The employer must receive a verified negative drug screen before allowing any employee to perform safety-sensitive duties.

Alcohol Testing

Alcohol testing is an integral part of a successful drug and alcohol testing program. Because alcohol is a legal substance, alcohol testing is authorized on during, just before, or just after the employee performs safety-sensitive functions.
